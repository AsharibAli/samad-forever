What is Forever Living Products?

Forever Living Products is a multi-level marketing company that manufactures and sells aloe vera-based drinks and bee-derived cosmetics, nutritional supplements, and personal care products. The company was founded in 1978 by Rex Maughan and is headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona.

Comapany History:

Founded in 1978 by Rex Maughan. Started with a focus on aloe vera drinks and gels. By the early 1980s expanded to include bee products and nutritional supplements. Went international in 1982, opening in Canada. Now operates in over 150 countries worldwide. Reported sales revenue of $2.5 billion in 2017. Employs over 10,000 people globally. Maughan stepped down as CEO in 2002 but remained Chairman of the Board.

Products they're selling:

  • Forever Living Products has four main product lines: Nutritional supplements - Vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, herbs. Weight management - Meal replacement shakes, pills, teas. Personal care - Soaps, lotions, cosmetics, essential oils. Bee products - Bee pollen, royal jelly, propolis.

Forever Living Products: An Overview & Business Model

  • Business Model

    Forever Living employs a multi-level marketing model. Individuals sign up as distributors and earn income both from direct sales and by recruiting others into their "downlines."

  • Marketing Campaigns

    The focus is driving personal sales through relationship-based, word-of-mouth marketing versus traditional print/media advertising. The MLM model provides a salesforce to spread brand awareness.

  • Distributors

    Distributors purchase products wholesale and sell at retail price. Commission on personal and team sales range from 5-43% based on volume. Higher rankings (e.g. Supervisor, Assistant Manager) earn higher bonuses. Awards and incentives for top performers, including vacations and luxury goods.

  • Control of Legal Issues

    The company has faced some legal issues over its business structure but maintains its focus on natural health products.
